PRE-K

(4 years old)

Welcome to the 4-K Program at Brainorama, where we provide a stimulating and nurturing environment for children aged 4 years, preparing them for a smooth transition into kindergarten and laying a solid foundation for lifelong learning. Our program is designed to foster curiosity, creativity, social-emotional development, and academic readiness through engaging activities and supportive interactions.

Experienced and Dedicated Educators

Our 4-K Program is led by experienced educators who are passionate about early childhood education. They create a positive and inclusive atmosphere where every child feels valued and supported in their learning journey. Our teachers are committed to providing personalized attention and fostering a love for learning in each child.

Safe and Stimulating Learning Environment

Safety is our utmost priority. Our classrooms are carefully designed with age-appropriate materials, learning centers, and comfortable spaces that promote exploration and discovery. We maintain high standards of cleanliness and hygiene to ensure a healthy environment where children can learn, play, and grow.

Comprehensive Curriculum

Our curriculum is thoughtfully designed to support all areas of development. We integrate play-based learning with structured activities that promote language and literacy skills, mathematical thinking, scientific exploration, creative arts, and social studies. Children engage in hands-on experiences that encourage critical thinking, problem-solving, and collaboration.

Daily Schedule and Activities

Our daily schedule includes a balance of teacher-led activities, small group work, independent exploration, and outdoor play. Preparation for Kindergarten

The 4-K Program focuses on preparing children for a successful transition to kindergarten. We emphasize important skills such as phonemic awareness, letter recognition, counting, number sense, fine and gross motor skills, self-regulation, and social interaction. Our educators provide differentiated instruction to meet the needs of each child and ensure readiness for the next educational milestone.
