Toddlers

(2 year old)

Welcome to the Toddlers Program at Brainorama, where curiosity meets exploration and every day is filled with wonder and discovery. Our program is designed specifically for kids aged 2 years, focusing on nurturing their growing independence, social skills, and cognitive development in a safe and stimulating environment.

Experienced and Dedicated Educators

Our Toddler Program is led by caring and experienced educators who understand the unique needs of toddlers. They create a supportive atmosphere where toddlers feel secure to explore and learn at their own pace. Our team fosters a love for learning through engaging activities that encourage curiosity and creativity.

Safe and Engaging Environment

Safety is paramount. Our toddler rooms are designed with safety features and age-appropriate furniture to promote independence while ensuring a secure space for exploration and play. We maintain cleanliness and hygiene standards to provide a healthy environment for our toddlers.

Play-Based Learning

Learning through play is at the core of our Toddler Program. We offer a balanced curriculum that includes structured activities and ample opportunities for free play. Toddlers engage in sensory play, art exploration, music and movement, storytelling, and outdoor activities that stimulate their cognitive, social-emotional, and physical development.

Daily Routine and Activities

Our daily routine is structured yet flexible to accommodate the individual needs and rhythms of toddlers. It includes group activities for social interaction, circle time for stories and songs, snack time for practicing independence and fine motor skills, nap time for rest and rejuvenation, and outdoor play to promote gross motor skills and exploration of nature.

Developmental Milestones

The Toddler Program focuses on supporting key developmental milestones such as language acquisition, problem-solving skills, self-help skills (like feeding and toileting), and building positive relationships with peers and adults.
